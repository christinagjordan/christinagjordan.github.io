how I started my first busines

Discover how a childhood dream of baking, fueled by creativity and family needs, evolved into a thriving business helping others create joyful, safe memories in the kitchen.

My entrepreneurial journey began at the tender age of nine with my first venture, Ellie World Creations. I had always dreamed of owning a bakery, but quickly learned that securing a license at that age was, understandably, out of the question. Undeterred, I pivoted my passion into something tangible: selling handmade soaps, delightful bath bombs, and charmingly punny baked-goods t-shirts. My amazing mom, seeing my drive, enrolled me in a six-week entrepreneurship bootcamp, which armed me with the foundational skills I needed. Just two weeks after my ninth birthday, I proudly debuted Ellie World Creations at a local "Lemonade Stand Festival," my very own grand opening.

As I grew, so did my business ideas. By middle school, my experiences with family food allergies (gluten and nuts, specifically) inspired a new direction. I published *The Ellie World Cookbook and Creations*, a gluten- and nut-free cookbook designed to bring delicious, safe recipes to tables just like ours. This cookbook wasn’t just a project; it was the essential foundation that laid the groundwork for what would eventually become Honey Bunny Originals. It taught me the importance of catering to specific needs and creating inclusive joy through food.

Launched in 2022, Honey Bunny Originals is the culmination of those early dreams and lessons. Our mission is simple: to offer gluten-free, nut-free, and vegan cookie mixes that make safe, joyful family baking accessible to everyone. We want to remove the stress from baking for dietary restrictions, so all you have to do is add eggs and butter to our mixes, and you’re on your way to creating delicious memories. The joy of a freshly baked cookie, without the worry, is what we strive for.

From those early days of vending at local fairs to now running an SEO-optimized Etsy shop, I’ve learned invaluable lessons about the ebb and flow of business. I’ve navigated the need to pivot with seasons, adapt to new regulations, and even found creative ways to fund growth – like selling Canva templates I’d created from past school projects. Through every iteration and every challenge, my core goal has always remained the same: helping people create sweet, lasting memories through delicious food, no matter their dietary needs. It’s a journey that started with a simple dream and continues to be fueled by passion.
