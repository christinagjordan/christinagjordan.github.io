**how I started my first business**

My entrepreneurial journey began at age nine, transforming a dream of owning a bakery into a multi-faceted business that now helps families bake safely.

Like many nine-year-olds, I dreamed big. My ultimate fantasy was owning a bakery, but a quick reality check (and my mom explaining licensing laws) showed me that wasn't quite in the cards yet. Unfazed, I pivoted. I wanted to create things people loved, and that spark led to the birth of my very first business, Ellie World Creations. We started small, but mighty, offering homemade soaps, luxurious bath bombs, and even punny baked-goods t-shirts for those who shared my sweet tooth spirit.

My mom, always my biggest supporter, enrolled me in a fantastic six-week entrepreneurship bootcamp, which armed me with the foundational skills I needed. Just two weeks after my ninth birthday, armed with my handcrafted goods and a whole lot of enthusiasm, I proudly debuted my brand at a local “Lemonade Stand Festival.” It was exhilarating to see people enjoy what I had poured my heart into, and it solidified my passion for bringing joy to others through creative ventures.

As I grew, so did my business and my understanding of what truly mattered. By middle school, inspired by my family's own food allergies, I realized there was a deeper need I could address. This led me to publish *The Ellie World Cookbook and Creations*, a gluten- and nut-free cookbook packed with recipes designed to be safe and delicious for everyone. This pivotal project wasn't just about sharing recipes; it laid the essential groundwork for what would become my current venture, Honey Bunny Originals.

Launched in 2022, Honey Bunny Originals is a natural evolution of that original dream, now with an even clearer mission. We offer thoughtfully crafted gluten-free, nut-free, and vegan cookie mixes, making safe and joyful family baking accessible to everyone—you just add eggs and butter. It’s all about creating those precious, sweet memories without the worry of dietary restrictions.

Throughout my journey, from excitedly vending at local fairs to meticulously running an SEO-optimized Etsy shop, I’ve learned invaluable lessons. I've learned to pivot with seasons and regulations, to listen to my customers, and even to be resourceful, selling Canva templates from past school projects to help fund the business's growth. Every step, every challenge, has been a learning opportunity. My goal, however, has always remained steadfast and simple: helping people create sweet, lasting memories through delicious food and shared experiences, no matter their dietary needs.
