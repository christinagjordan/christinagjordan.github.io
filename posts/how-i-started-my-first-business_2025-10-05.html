how I started my first business

My path from handmade creations as a child to a gluten- and nut-free baking business is a journey of innovation and joy.

the evolution from Ellieworld to Honey Bunny



Growing up, I always envisioned myself running a bakery, but at the tender age of nine, getting a food license was, understandably, out of reach. Undeterred, I channeled my entrepreneurial spirit into my very first venture: Ellie World Creations. My product line was an eclectic mix of soaps, bath bombs, and whimsical t-shirts featuring punny baked-goods designs. It was my way of bringing joy and creativity to others, even if I couldn't whip up real cookies yet.

My incredibly supportive mom played a pivotal role in getting Ellie World Creations off the ground. She enrolled me in a fantastic six-week entrepreneurship bootcamp, which armed me with the foundational skills I needed. Ms. Kami, the lady who ran the program, taught us how to take an idea and make it reality. I learned about creating a business plan and a formulating a strong elevator pitch for investors (though I insisted it was called an “elephant” pitch). Right before I celebrated my ninth birthday, armed with my handmade wares and a burgeoning business plan, I proudly debuted my brand at the culminating event of the bootcamp called the “Lemonade Stand Festival.” It was exhilarating to see people I had never met before engage with my creations and make my very first sales.

As I moved into middle school, my passion for creating was regrounded in my dream of using my baking to make a impact on the world. Inspired by my family’s own experiences with food allergies, I began focusing more on safe, delicious recipes. This led to the publication of The Ellie World Cookbook and Creations, a comprehensive guide filled with gluten- and nut-free recipes. This cookbook wasn't just a fun project; it laid the essential groundwork and provided the inspiration for what would become my most significant business venture yet.

Launched in 2022, Honey Bunny Originals is dedicated to making baking accessible and joyful for everyone. We offer delicious gluten-free, nut-free, and vegan cookie mixes that make safe family baking incredibly easy – all you need to add are eggs and butter. Our mixes ensure that no one misses out on the fun and deliciousness of homemade treats, regardless of their dietary needs.

My journey from Ellie World Creations to Honey Bunny Originals has been a fantastic learning curve. I’ve gone from vending at local fairs to running an SEO-optimized Etsy shop, learning to pivot with seasons and regulations. I even got creative when I realized that I was selling less cookie mixes outside of the fall and winter months by selling Canva templates from past school projects to reinvest in the business. Every step has taught me invaluable lessons about business, creativity, and resilience.

Through it all, my core goal has remained the same: helping people create sweet, lasting memories, no matter their dietary needs. It's about bringing families together in the kitchen and at the table, ensuring that everyone can enjoy the simple, profound joy of a freshly baked cookie.
